About me

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m Devon, a dedicated web developer with a passion for creating functional, user-friendly websites. My journey into the world of coding began on a smaller scale, but over time, I’ve honed my skills and continuously push myself to learn new technologies and techniques that elevate my work.

Before diving into web development, I wore a few different hats. I started out as a carpenter, where I learned the value of precision and craftsmanship. After that, I spent some time as a truck driver, navigating long stretches of highway and gaining a strong sense of discipline and responsibility. These diverse experiences have shaped my approach to development, blending creativity, problem-solving, and a hands-on attitude.

One thing that drives me is the constant pursuit of knowledge. I thrive on learning new things, whether it’s a new programming language, design principle, or an emerging trend in the tech world. This hunger for knowledge keeps me motivated and ensures that my work is always evolving.

Now, as a full-time developer, I’m focused on bringing my ideas to life through code. While I don’t have pets to distract me, my time is fully dedicated to perfecting my craft and staying up to date with the latest industry trends.
